Matthew Edwards

Welcome to my professional website! My name's Matt, and I work as an Economist for the Regional Municipality of York (just north of Toronto). My current work deals mostly with inflation and the labour market. I'm also interested in fiscal and monetary policy (particularly as they relate to consumer inflation). Before starting my current role, I studied statistics at the University of Toronto, where I focused on statistical machine learning and simulation. Before that I worked as a researcher for the Government of Canada, after studying Economics at Queen's University. For a more detailed bio, please see my resume above.

Below are some of my recent projects, which are mostly on Math and Econ topics. Besides research, I enjoy technical writing and education (see the blog above). If you'd like to discuss my work (or just want to talk data), feel free to reach out to me (I prefer email, see below).
